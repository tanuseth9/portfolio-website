About Me

Hi, I'm Tanu Seth! I did my bachelors in accounting from India and after that, I joined a hospital. I have more than 7 years of experience in
healthcare in customer service. Then we moved to the USA and I decided to further my studies. I have completed one year certification in
Application Development from Renton Technical College, Washington with 3.9 GPA. This motivated me even further to follow my passion and
being a creative and visual person I wanted to go into UX/UI. Recently I graduated from CareerFoundry, where I studied UX methodologies
and learnt fundamentals of UI design, Front-end web development and Accessibility through hands-on projects. I created 3 web apps end to
end from ideation to final visual design.

Having the background of customer service in healthcare as well as experience as an educator, I empathize with users better as it helps me
uncover their needs and pain points that can enhance the overall user experience. I believe that the best way to grow is to stay current,
relevant and curious to learn new things and exchange ideas with others. Incorporating both my customer service specialty and creativity, I
want to build satisfying product experiences and solutions.

In addition to all this, I have an interest in painting, voiceover, and movie editing, I have given my voice to children book, stories and did
editing for some short movies and children stories.
