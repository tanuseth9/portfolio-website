Hi, I am Tanu Seth!

Welcome to my portfolio. I am a Product Designer (UX/UI) with an
education in computer science and technology focused on creating
experiences that are meaningful and valuable for a customer with
mobile first approach in mind. I aim for the entire user experience to
be inclusive, appealing and user centered. I involve users throughout
the design process via a variety of research and design techniques to
create highly usable and accessible product.
